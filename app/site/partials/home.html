Mentorship Program

Our Mentorship Program is designed to help guide students through each step involved in making a succesful transition from high school to University. Upon joining you will be paired with a mentor from one of your top schools of choice. He or she will meet up with you in regular intervals throughout the year to discuss schools, programs, campus life and your applications.

Mentorship Program Overview

1:1 Student-Mentor Ratio

We ensure a good match with a mentor from one of your top schools of choice.

Access to our Mentor Network

You will be granted access to our entire network of mentors and be given the opportunity to reach out to them and inquire about other schools and programs.

Essay Writing

With coaching and assistance throughout the writing process,Â you will learn proper essay and application structure.

Interview Prep

Your mentor will go over the most commonly asked interview questions, conducting mock interviews with you to ensure that you are well prepared for the process.

A Step-by-Step Process

Choosing a School and Program

Your mentor will take you through the necessary steps to help you choose which schools and programs are a good fit for you. You will be given the chance to draw from your mentor's first-hand experiences in an open setting. he two of you will discuss everything from campus life to career prospects. You will also be invited to reach out to our mentor network to discuss similar topics with mentors from other schools and programs.

Preparation and Planning of your Application

You will work with your mentor to carefully plan and outline the structure of your application. You will be advised on proper writing tools and techniques to ensure your application stands out from the crowd.

Writing, Revision and Final Submission

You will receive feedback throughout the entire writing process, with notes, comments, and editorial assistance. When it's time for final submission you will be ready with an application perfectly tailored for the school and program to which you are applying

University Prep 101

Once admitted to University your mentor will take the time to discuss courses, residences, study tips, and general first year advice with you. You will also be added to our alumni network where you can begin to connect with other first year students well before the start of school.

Application Process

1. Online Application

Submit your application to give us a chance to get to know you a little better!

2. Chat With a Team Member

One of our team members will reach out to discuss your application and how we intend to help you get most out of your mentorship.

3. Meet Your Mentor

You will be integrated into our communication platform, it's time for you and your mentor to connect!
